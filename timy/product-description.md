# Product Vision Canvas

## Vision Statement
Timy makes time tracking effortless by turning everyday communication into structured, accurate timesheets without requiring another app.

## Target Users

### Time Reporters
- employees
- contractors
- freelancers

### Budget Owners
- project managers
- team leads
- department heads responsible for budgets and capacity.

## User Needs

### Time Reporters
- Log hours quickly and naturally
- No new tools, accounts, or passwords
- Freedom in format and language
- Easy corrections

### Budget Owners
- Complete and timely time entries
- Accurate budget and burn visibility
- Fewer late or missing submissions
- Less manual follow-up

## Key Features
- Time entry and insights via any channel (email, WhatsApp, Signal, SMS, etc.)
- Natural language understanding (any format, any language)
- Automatic structuring and system registration
- Automated clarification when entries are unclear
- Corrections and updates via the same channel
- Smart reminders for missing time

## Business Goals (Measurable)
- Increase percentage of on-time submissions
- Reduce late and missing entries
- Reduce manual corrections and follow-ups
- Improve user satisfaction (eNPS)
- Reduce administrative processing time

# Product principles

## 1. No New Behavior
Users should not have to learn a new tool or change how they work. Timy adapts to existing communication habits.

## 2. Invisible by Design
The best interaction is no interaction. Timy works in the background and only surfaces when needed.

## 3. Clarity Over Complexity
Time entry should feel simple and human. The system handles structure and rules, not the user.

## 4. Proactive, Not Reactive
Timy follows up automatically when something is unclear or missing, reducing manual chasing.

## 5. Structured at the Core
Every input, regardless of format or channel, becomes reliable, structured, system-ready data.

## 6. Correction Without Friction
Users can update or fix entries through the same channel, as easily as sending a message.

## 7. System-Agnostic Integration
Timy integrates with existing tools and workflows rather than replacing them.

## 8. Trust Through Transparency
Users can always see, confirm, and understand how their time was interpreted and registered.

## 9. Reduce Administrative Waste
Every feature must reduce friction, manual work, or cognitive load.

## Time Reporters

1. When I finish a workday or week,
   I want to quickly capture what I worked on,
   so I can stay compliant without interrupting my flow.

2. When I remember work I forgot to log,
   I want to update it easily,
   so my records stay accurate without hassle.

3. When submitting time,
   I want it to be interpreted correctly the first time,
   so I don’t have to re-enter or clarify later.

4. When deadlines approach,
   I want simple reminders,
   so I avoid stress and last-minute corrections.

5. When reporting hours,
   I want the process to feel lightweight,
   so I don’t feel frustrated by administrative work.


## Budget Owners

1. When managing a project,
   I want all time submitted on time and structured,
   so I can trust budget and burn visibility.

2. When entries are missing or unclear,
   I want automatic follow-up,
   so I don’t spend time chasing people.

3. When reviewing project hours,
   I want reliable, standardized data,
   so I can make confident financial decisions.

4. When reporting upward,
   I want accurate time data,
   so I appear in control of budgets and timelines.
